Technical Specification Handbook

Core Platform Capabilities

A deep dive into the 12 core requirements, system architectures, and lifecycle operations governing the MYHitch Lens platform.

01 / Specification

User Management

Governs how readers, vetted contributors, and publishers interact with the publishing ecosystem.

  • User Registration & Profiles: Flexible onboarding profiles supporting customized reader interests, author portfolio bios, and corporate publisher listings.
  • Verified Authors: Domain experts go through academic and professional vetting checks to earn a verified contribution checkmark badge.
  • Contributor ranking: Authors earn score points based on article view counts, positive comment reviews, and validation logs, progressing from Bronze to Silver and Gold status tiers.
02 / Specification

Subscription Plans

The foundation of the platform's access control and contributor payout monetization engine.

  • Reader Subscription ($9.99/mo): Grants full access to clean publication articles, commenting directories, bookmark folders, and customized alerts.
  • Author Subscription ($29.99/mo): Enables verified badge vetting access, full AI assistant checking tools, SEO reports, and access to payout revenue shares.
  • Corporate Publisher ($199/mo): Grants access to team collaboration accounts, report publication catalogs, and connected MYHitch procurement links.
03 / Specification

Article Submission

The author's content drafting workspace, supporting diverse media formats.

  • Rich Text Editor: Features formatted structures, blockquotes, code fences, and links.
  • PDF & Word parser: Features a drag-and-drop file ingestion module that parses documents, automatically loading titles and drafting text.
  • Media Attachments: Full support for embedding infographics, short videos, audio tracks, and PDF research references.
04 / Specification

Editorial Workflow

The lifecycle tracking content progress from draft to public availability.

1. Draft Created → 2. AI Validation → 3. Editorial Queue → 4. Scheduled Publish
  • Vetted Authors: Submit draft documents. AI scanning is triggered instantly to ensure plagiarism is below compliance thresholds.
  • Human Editors: Reviews drafts, provides feedback, requests revisions, or publishes content.
05 / Specification

Editorial Dashboard

The control room for moderators and publisher admins to schedule and curate content feeds.

  • Review Queue: A centralized queue listing drafts with automated AI quality checkpoints and assignee selectors.
  • Scheduling: Select release dates and times for posts to automate release calendars.
  • Moderation: Review reader comment feeds, resolve disputes, and manage compliance flags.
06 / Specification

Content Categories

The taxonomical index organizing publications across 11 key operational sectors.

  • Core Sectors: AI, Supply Chain, Technology, Business, Finance, Healthcare, Education, Travel, Research, Lifestyle, and Community.
  • Dynamic Filtering: Allows readers to instantly filter the feed using sector tags or category grid cards.
07 / Specification

Revenue Model

How the ecosystem pays vetting costs and remunerates quality authors.

  • Payout Engine: Direct revenue shares allocated to verified authors based on reading time metrics.
  • Corporate Reports: Allows publishers to sell premium industry reports under digital token structures.
  • Programmatic Ads: Ad space revenue divided between the platform and content creators.
08 / Specification

AI Features

A suite of AI assistants to help format and optimize content.

  • Plagiarism Validation: Semantic pattern checks ensuring high authenticity scores.
  • Translation & Voice Synthesis: Multi-language translation matrices and voice narrators translating text to speech.
  • Summarization: Abstract summarizer generation to outline key bullet points instantly.
09 / Specification

Reader Features

Enhances engagement and personalization for platform subscribers.

  • Reading Options: Bookmarks, Likes, and comment discussions.
  • Follow Creators: Follow favorite vetted contributors to populate customized notifications feeds.
  • Preferences: Dark Mode toggles for optimal readability.
10 / Specification

Analytics

Telemetry indicators tracking publication reach and subscriber metrics.

  • Views & Read Time: Tracks individual reading durations and total view graphs.
  • SVG Dashboards: Modern line charts and bar distributions tracking performance metrics in real-time.
11 / Specification

Governance & Policy

Maintains credibility, copyrights, and editorial standards.

  • Double-Blind peer reviews: Restricts identity disclosure during publication vetting cycles.
  • DRM copyright tokens: Digital rights management keys protecting original texts against scrapers.
  • Appeals Intake: Dispute tickets allowing authors to appeal moderator rejections.
12 / Specification

MYHitch Integrations

Connected API modules extending the usefulness of publications.

  • Mart procurement links: Instantly displays physical products or hardware modules mentioned in articles.
  • JetNRest travel SAF: Embeds carbon-offset flight search buttons inside travel operations articles.
  • Consulting Schedulers & Wallet Donations: Connects scheduling systems and web3 donation micro-grants directly into article footers.
